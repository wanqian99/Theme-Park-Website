FAQ

What are the theme park's opening hours?

We are open from All Week from 12PM - 9PM.
Where is the theme park located?

We are located at Senate House Malet Street London WC1E 7HU.
Is there a designated pram deposition location?

Yes you can deposit your pram at a counter found beside each ride.
I am a vegan, vegetarian, muslim, have allergies or have any other specific dietary requirements.

We have a wide range of food choices to meet the dietary requirements of all our diverse guest. More information can be found in our Dining page.
I will be visiting with a disabled person. What services do you provide?.

We have many fun non-thriller rides and attractions to ensure all our guest feel welcomed. There are no stairs in the park so accessiblity is smooth. If you require assistance with mobility, just approach any of our staff to help you.
Can tickets be refunded?

If you make a refund request within a week of purchase, we will give you a full refund.
What measures are taken during wet weather?

All our ride are weatherproof and safe to ride even in wet weather. We offer free ponchos at any counter and sell exclusive design umbrellas at our merchandise shops.
Can I bring my own food?

Yes, you can. Just don't eat on the rides :)
Are there rules for viewing the parades and shows?

Please stay clear of the main roads and the entourage and carry your young children, as there are moving vehicles that can cause bodily harm.
I wish to celebrate a birthday or anniversary at the park. Do you offer any special celebratory options?

Do send us an email at Ask@camphalfblood.com
