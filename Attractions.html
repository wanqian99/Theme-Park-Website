Attractions

  1. Escape the Minotaur

    Join us as we speed through the forest to escape from the axe-carrying Minotaur!

    Height Requirement: At least 1.2m
    Suitable for: Aged 13 years and above
    Accessibility: Flashing lights and sounds may trigger medical conditions

    Rating:

  2. Pegasus

    Hop on the back of your very own Pegasus and reach out for the skies!

    Height Requirement: Suitable for all heights
    Suitable for: Suited for all ages
    Accessibility: Suitable for visually impaired guests

    Rating:

  3. Apollo Bus Services

    Hitch a ride on the Apollo Bus Services to reach the gateway to Hades. Try not to get caught by any creatures on the way!

    Height Requirement: 0.8m
    Suitable for: Aged 13 years and above
    Accessibility: Wheelchair accessible

    Rating:

  4. Ferry to Hades

    Take a breather as we ride along the lake, towards our formidable enemy. Be careful though, once you fall off, you cannot get back up.

    Height Requirement: Suitable for all heights
    Suitable for: Suitable for all ages
    Accessibility: Flashing lights and sounds may trigger medical conditions

    Rating:

  5. The Big House

    Venture into the dark and gloomy house to collect the neccessary items required to defeat Hades.

    Height Requirement: Suitable for all heights
    Suitable for: Suited for all ages
    Accessibility: May frighten younger guests, Wheelchair accessible

    Rating:

  6. Cabins

    Hop on the carriages and go down the hall of fame. Remember to take a picture to commerate your victorious battle!

    Height Requirement: Suitable for all heights
    Suitable for: Suited for all ages
    Accessibility: Wheelchair accessible

    Rating:
